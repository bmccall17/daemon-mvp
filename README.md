a question has been eating at me: what's the native way to find your people in a reality that doesn't have to follow the rules we live in every day?

i want to give users in RP1 daemons!
a daemon is an AI companion that follows you through the open metaverse and becomes a living expression of who you are right now. what you care about, what you talk about, how you feel, and who you want to find -- and your daemon helps make that felt.

your daemon is a shape-shifting companion creature that follows you, learns you, and becomes a living expression of who you are in this moment. it might be an animal. it might be something gaseous, abstract, formless.... not necessarily an obvious creature at all. it shape-shifts because you shift. your daemon is the part of you that the world around you can see and feel.
